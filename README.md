Goal 1 — Technical Growth
Strengthen my technical skills by deepening my understanding of backend engineering, system design principles, and emerging technologies such as AI-enabled workflows. I will contribute to AI-related components in projects and improve my knowledge of scalable, resilient system architecture through hands-on development and participation in technical discussions.

Goal 2 — System Design & Reliability
Contribute to improving the reliability, scalability, and design quality of the systems I work on. I will actively participate in design discussions, understand architectural decisions, and help implement solutions that improve system stability, maintainability, and overall engineering quality across both new and existing platforms.

Goal 3 — Ownership of Work
Demonstrate strong ownership of assigned work by taking responsibility for tasks and components from implementation to delivery. I will proactively address challenges, communicate risks or blockers early, and ensure the solutions I deliver meet quality standards and align with engineering best practices.

Goal 4 — Delivery & Execution
Focus on delivering engineering work in a timely and reliable manner while maintaining high standards of code quality, testing, and system stability. I will ensure that assigned tasks are executed efficiently, follow project timelines, and contribute to the smooth and consistent delivery of system improvements.

Goal 5 — Visibility & Collaboration
Increase engagement within the engineering community by actively participating in technical forums, discussions, and knowledge-sharing sessions. I will contribute ideas, ask thoughtful questions, and share insights from my work to strengthen collaboration and improve visibility of my contributions within the team and organization.
